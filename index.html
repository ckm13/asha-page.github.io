What are we all about?

Considered Consumerism is a resource for the conscious fashion lover who wants to shop smart. We aim to spread awareness of the disruptors in the industry, creating beautiful things that don't cost the earth.

Ethical Shopping Suggestions

Reformation

According to Reformation being naked is the no.1 sustainable option and they are no.2. With an ethos of transparency and accountability at every stage of their supply chain, Reformation are an industry leader in the fight against fast fashion. Reformation focus on feminine silhouettes and breezy fabrics, Reformation is for those who want to embody effortless chic without compromising their carbon footprint.

Kotn.

Kotn. is a sustainable fashion label for those who live in their basics. Manufactured from Egyptian Cotton, Kotn. produce premium pieces such as cloud soft t-shirts and turtlenecks.
